GNU/Linux + Free/Libre & Open Source Software + Fun

We are a group of technology and Free software enthusiasts who live in and around Chicago, IL. While our group primarily focuses on GNU/Linux, our members have a broad range of interests and experience, and our ranks include users of all major (and less major) operating systems.

Special Announcement

With the help of the Free Software Foundation, former Chicagoan Chris Webber recently announced a fundraising compaign to support work on GNU MediaGoblin, a Free software media-hosting platform.

However, the campaign is ending on Friday! If you can spare a few dollars, please consider giving a donation to this great, locally-grown Free software project. Even if you can't spare a few bucks, though, have a look at the website and video to learn more about what Chris and the team are doing to support Free and Open Source software on the web. This video explains things even more! Thanks!
